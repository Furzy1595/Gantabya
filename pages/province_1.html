Vibrant Tapestry of Culture

Province 1 boasts a rich cultural heritage with diverse ethnic groups and traditions. Here's a glimpse:

  • Experience the joyous celebrations of Udhauli (Maghe Sankranti) and Dhan Nach (Rice Planting Festival).
  • Witness the captivating Limbu dances like Chyabrung and Dhol, pulsating with energy.
  • Marvel at the intricate textiles woven by the Rai community, showcasing their artistry.
  • Explore the traditional Nepali architecture with intricate wood carvings, a testament to craftsmanship.
  • Visit the numerous Hindu and Buddhist pilgrimage sites, steeped in spiritual significance.
